# Enterprise Applications

Enterprise applications (also known as information systems or data processing systems) are a specific category of software with unique challenges.

## Characteristics
- **Persistent Data**: Usually involves a large amount of data that must persist across sessions and multiple years.
- **Concurrent Access**: Many people access and manipulate data at the same time.
- **Complex Rules**: Business rules often lack pure logic and are shaped by regulation, history, or business strategy.
- **Integration**: Often need to integrate with other enterprise systems (legacy or new).

## Examples
- Payroll, patient records, shipping tracking, cost analysis, credit scoring, insurance, supply chain, accounting, customer service, and international trade.

## What they are NOT
- Fuel injection systems, word processors, elevators, chemical plant controllers, or flight simulators.
