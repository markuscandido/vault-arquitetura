# Architecture

Architecture is a subjective term in software engineering, often used to denote "important things" about a system's design.

## Definitions
1. **High-level Decomposition**: The breakdown of a system into its major parts.
2. **Hard-to-Change Decisions**: Decisions that are difficult to alter once implementation has progressed.
3. **Shared Understanding**: (Ralph Johnson) A system's design shared by the expert developers on a project.

## Subjectivity
Architecture depends on what is perceived as difficult to change. If a decision is found to be easily reversible, it may lose its "architectural" status.
